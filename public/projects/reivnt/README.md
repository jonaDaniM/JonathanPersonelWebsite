# Reivnt Public Image Placeholders

Place public-safe Reivnt duplex images in this directory.

Expected filenames currently referenced by the case study:

- `exterior-before.jpg`
- `exterior-after.jpg`
- `interior-before.jpg`
- `interior-after.jpg`
- `framing.jpg`
- `finished-kitchen.jpg`

Before publishing images, verify they do not show:

- Tenant information
- Street addresses
- License plates
- Private financial or closing documents
- Contractor personal information
- Anything that should remain private for safety, legal, or business reasons
